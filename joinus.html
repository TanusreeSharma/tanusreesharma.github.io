We are looking for highly-motivated PhD, Masters, and Undergraduate students with interests in: usable security & privacy, blockchain, HCI, and accessible computing.

What Skills Are We Looking For?
  • Design Skill: Experience in design and iterative prototyping (low/high-fidelity) is highly valued.
  • Coding Skill: We build mobile, web, and browser-extension tools. Full-stack developers are especially welcome.
  • Domain Knowledge: Familiarity with privacy/security, blockchain, or AI will be beneficial. Prior publications are a bonus but not required.
