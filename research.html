Governing LLM Models with Democratic Decision-Making

This line of work introduces a paradigm for AI governance involving diverse stakeholders to collaboratively address social, technical, and political challenges through Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) mechanisms. This includes various decision-making schemes like quadratic, ranking, and weighted voting, with implementening tangible way to distribute power to community to participate in decision-making regarding to enable democratization in AI. Featured project -- (1) builing decentralization and autonomy metrics; (2) building tool, Inclusive.AI to improve user involvement in governing large language models and shaping future policies.

Visual Privacy Management for Blind Users

This line of work includes creation of method and tooling for dataset for AI development for underserved group (e.g. blind users)in ethical way. Main goal is Empowering individuals to identify and manage any private content found in their images and videos. Two featured project includes:
(1) novel data creation/collection method for ethical AI development pipeline; a new dataset "BivPriv" containing private content created with blind users; benchmarking dataset for novel algorithm development.
(2) A prototype, inspired by blind users' preferences, automatically identifies private objects in photos and lets users choose if and how to hide them. It features a user-friendly interface optimized for screen readers, with two main sections: "explore image" and "edit image"

Decentralized Application Usability, Security & Accessibility

This line of work includes usable privacy and security of Blockchain and decentralized application for addressing issues around both end users and developers' security and privacy. Some Featured Projects involves--
(1) Designing tools for secure coding of smart contracts with a mixed Method approach with smart contract developers. practices
(2) Iterative Design and Development of iWallet - a crypto wallet with accessibility, education, and usable security features for blind users. A work contributed to industry stakeholders
(3) Design and Evaluation of cryptographic protocol, MFKDF in the context of crypto wallets for usable trustless key management with a potential to benefit various decentralized applications. As a result of the efforts, this project lead to the developed the Ciao wallet

Privacy In Global South

This line of work is motivated by the cultural, economic, and development differences in the Global South, where the Western definition of privacy is often not applicable. As a result of this project, we found novel user mental models of targeted ads based on mobile app permissions and excessive AI usage. People from Global South often preferred ad control over transparency. Another series of project under this line of work involves exploring and designing ad technology, emerging crypto technology, AI, etc, and technologies used in crisis situation, such as COVID-19.

Privacy Principle & Regulation

Data is a valuable asset for organizations in data-driven economies, but without effective measures, data misuse can negatively impact end-users. The 2018 GDPR gives individuals control over their personal data. This project aims to explore users' understanding of data minimization in search engines, factors influencing their decision to share search data, and their perspective on how data minimization should work in AI System.
