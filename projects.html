Current Research Focus

My research focuses on privacy security and human-computer interaction. I study people's attitudes, concerns, decision-making, and behaviors to (1) address emerging privacy and security issues to develop usable solutions, enabling better control of their information in the digital world; and (2) develop methods and tools for engaging diverse stakeholders at scale to co-design privacy and governance solutions. My research often involves a mixed-method approach with an iterative design process that consists of studying users, building prototypes, and evaluating them empirically. I employ both qualitative and quantitative methods such as surveys, interviews, internet measurement, experiments, server log analyses, Internet measurement studies and machine learning.

Three main lines of research:

  • usable privacy and security: designing usable and effective mechanisms to support people with different backgrounds and characteristics to protect their privacy and security
  • usable crypto/blockchain: designing and evaluating decentralized mechanisms to improve the risk, security, and trust perception of end users
  • AI Governance: designing and developing novel tools and methods to engage diverse stakeholders in decision-making for high-stakes, data-driven application

Some Example Projects

Visual Privacy Management for Blind Users



Project Website

This line of work includes creation of method and tooling for dataset for AI development for underserved group (e.g. blind users)in ethical way and support individuals to identify and manage any private content in their images and videos. Two featured project includes:
(1) novel data creation method for ethical AI development pipeline;
(2) Prototypes to automatically identify private objects in photos to support blind users how to obfuscate them.

Related Publication

  • "Disability-First Design and Creation of A Dataset Showing Private Visual Information Collected With People Who Are Blind." CHI 202e [PDF]
  • Designing Accessible Obfuscation Support for Blind Individuals’ Visual Privacy Management. CHI 2024 [PDF]
  • BIV-Priv-Seg: Locating Private Content in Images Taken by People With Visual Impairments. PDF
  • GuardLens: Supporting Safer Online Browsing for People with Visual Impairments. SOUPS 2023 PDF
